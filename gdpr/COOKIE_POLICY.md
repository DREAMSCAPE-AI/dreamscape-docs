## 2. What Are Cookies?

**Cookies** are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. Cookies allow the website to recognize your device and remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and actions.

### Types of Cookies

**Session Cookies**: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. Used for essential functions like maintaining your login session.

**Persistent Cookies**: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period (or until you delete them). Used to remember your preferences and provide personalized experiences.

**First-Party Cookies**: Set by DreamScape directly. We have full control over these cookies.

**Third-Party Cookies**: Set by external services we use (e.g., payment processors, analytics providers). These are governed by the third party's privacy policy.

### Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we use:
- **Local Storage**: Browser storage for larger amounts of data (e.g., user preferences, consent records)
- **Session Storage**: Temporary browser storage that clears when the tab is closed
- **Web Beacons / Pixels**: Small invisible images used to track email opens and page views
- **Device Identifiers**: Unique identifiers for mobile devices (e.g., advertising IDs)

Throughout this policy, the term "cookies" includes all these similar technologies.

## 6. How to Manage Cookies

You have several options to control and manage cookies:

### 6.1 DreamScape Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our Platform, you will see a cookie consent banner with the following options:

**Accept All Cookies**: Enable all cookie categories (essential, functional, analytics, marketing)

**Reject Non-Essential Cookies**: Enable only essential cookies required for basic functionality

**Customize Preferences**: Choose which cookie categories to enable:
- Essential: Always active (cannot be disabled)
- Functional: Toggle on/off
- Analytics: Toggle on/off
- Marketing: Toggle on/off

**Accessing Cookie Settings Later**:
- Click "Cookie Settings" in the website footer
- Go to Settings > Privacy Dashboard > Cookie Preferences (when logged in)
- Click the cookie icon (üç™) in the bottom corner of any page

**Your Choices Are Saved**: Your cookie preferences are stored in the `cookie-consent` cookie and localStorage for 1 year.

### 6.2 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through settings. You can:
- Block all cookies
- Block third-party cookies only
- Delete cookies after each session
- Receive notifications when cookies are set

**Common Browsers**:
- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox**: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
- **Edge**: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

**Important**: Blocking all cookies may prevent you from using certain features of our Platform, such as staying logged in or completing bookings.

### 6.3 Mobile Devices

**iOS**:
- Settings > Safari > Block All Cookies

**Android**:
- Browser app > Settings > Site settings > Cookies

**Advertising IDs**:
- **iOS**: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking
- **Android**: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

### 6.4 Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Currently, there is no industry standard for how to respond to DNT signals. We do not respond to DNT signals, but you can control cookies through the methods described above.

### 6.5 Withdrawing Consent

You can withdraw your cookie consent at any time through:
- Cookie Settings banner (footer link)
- Privacy Dashboard in your account settings
- Contacting privacy@dreamscape.ai

When you withdraw consent:
- Non-essential cookies will stop being set
- Existing cookies will stop tracking new activity
- Previously collected data may be retained for the duration specified in our retention schedule

## 7. Cookie Consent and Storage

### How We Store Your Cookie Preferences

Your cookie consent choices are stored in two places:

**1. Browser Cookie** (`cookie-consent`):
- Stores your consent status for each category
- Duration: 1 year
- Format: JSON object

**2. localStorage** (if logged in):
- Key: `cookie-consent`
- Synced with your account preferences
- Persists across devices when you log in

### Consent Record

When you provide or withdraw consent, we record:
- Timestamp of consent/withdrawal
- Cookie categories consented to
- Platform version and cookie policy version
- IP address (for audit purposes)
- User ID (if logged in)

This information is stored in our database (`UserConsent` and `ConsentHistory` models) for 3 years as proof of consent compliance.

### Consent Renewal

We may ask you to renew your consent:
- When this Cookie Policy is updated with material changes
- When new cookie categories or purposes are introduced
- After 1 year (automatic prompt to review preferences)

## 10. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Here's what happens if you disable different cookie categories:

| Cookie Category | If Disabled |
