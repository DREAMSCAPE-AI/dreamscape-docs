## 1. Introduction

Welcome to DreamScape, an innovative AI-powered travel platform designed to help you discover, plan, and book unforgettable travel experiences.

At DreamScape, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our website, mobile applications, and services (collectively, the "Platform").

DreamScape SAS ("we," "us," or "our") is the data controller responsible for processing your personal data. We are committed to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws.

**Key Points**:
- We only collect data necessary to provide and improve our services
- You have full control over your data with easy-to-use privacy tools
- We use advanced security measures to protect your information
- We never sell your personal data to third parties
- You can exercise your privacy rights at any time

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@dreamscape.ai.

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

### 3.4 Marketing and Communication (Legal Basis: Consent)

- **Promotional Emails**: Send newsletters, special offers, and travel inspiration (only with your consent)

### 3.5 Security and Fraud Prevention (Legal Basis: Legitimate Interest / Legal Obligation)

- **Account Security**: Detect and prevent unauthorized access
- **Fraud Detection**: Identify suspicious transactions and booking patterns
- **Platform Security**: Protect against cyberattacks and abuse
- **Compliance**: Meet legal obligations for identity verification and anti-money laundering

### 3.6 Legal Compliance (Legal Basis: Legal Obligation)

- **Tax and Accounting**: Maintain financial records as required by law
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Comply with travel industry regulations
- **Legal Proceedings**: Respond to court orders, legal requests, and disputes

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---------|-------------|
| Account management, bookings, payments | **Contract** (Article 6(1)(b)) - Necessary to provide services you requested |
| AI recommendations, service improvement | **Legitimate Interest** (Article 6(1)(f)) - Enhancing user experience while respecting your rights |
| Marketing emails, personalized ads, analytics cookies | **Consent** (Article 6(1)(a)) - You can withdraw consent at any time |
| Security, fraud prevention | **Legitimate Interest** (Article 6(1)(f)) - Protecting our users and platform |
| Tax records, legal compliance | **Legal Obligation** (Article 6(1)(c)) - Required by law |

When we rely on legitimate interest, we have conducted a balancing test to ensure our interests do not override your fundamental rights and freedoms. You have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interest.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|------------------|--------|
| Account and profile data | Account lifetime + 30 days after deletion | Service delivery, user convenience |
| Travel preferences | Account lifetime (editable anytime) | Personalization |
| Search and browsing history | 2 years or until consent withdrawn | Analytics, recommendations |
| Booking and trip data | 3 years after trip completion | Customer service, dispute resolution |
| Payment transactions | 5 years from transaction date | Legal requirement (French accounting law) |
| Consent records | 3 years from consent/withdrawal | GDPR compliance (proof of consent) |
| Audit and security logs | 3 years | Security, fraud detection, compliance |
| Session data | 7 days (JWT expiration) or logout | Authentication |
| Backup data | 90 days | Disaster recovery |

After the retention period expires, we securely delete or anonymize your data. Payment transaction data is anonymized after the legal retention period (personal identifiers removed, transaction amounts retained for accounting).

You can request earlier deletion of certain data types via your privacy dashboard or by contacting us.

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 8.1 Right of Access (Article 15)

You have the right to:
- Confirm whether we process your personal data
- Access a copy of your personal data
- Receive information about how we process your data

**How to Exercise**: Log in to your account and visit Settings > Privacy Dashboard, or contact dpo@dreamscape.ai.

**Response Time**: Immediate for structured data via our API; within 30 days for comprehensive reports.

### 8.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You have the right to:
- Correct inaccurate personal data
- Complete incomplete personal data

**How to Exercise**: Update your information directly in Settings > Profile, or contact support@dreamscape.ai.

**Response Time**: Changes are reflected immediately.

### 8.3 Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (Article 17)

You have the right to:
- Request deletion of your personal data

**How to Exercise**: Go to Settings > Privacy Dashboard > Delete My Account, or email dpo@dreamscape.ai.

**Process**:
1. We verify your identity
2. Your account is deactivated immediately
3. Personal data is deleted or anonymized within 30 days
4. You receive confirmation via email

**Exceptions**: We may retain data when legally required (e.g., payment transactions for 5 years for tax purposes).

**Note**: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Backups are deleted according to our backup retention schedule (90 days).

### 8.4 Right to Restriction of Processing (Article 18)

You have the right to:
- Restrict how we process your data in certain circumstances

**How to Exercise**: Adjust consent toggles in Settings > Privacy Dashboard, or contact dpo@dreamscape.ai.

**Effect**: We will store your data but not actively process it for restricted purposes.

### 8.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You have the right to:
- Receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON)
- Transmit your data to another service provider

**How to Exercise**: Go to Settings > Privacy Dashboard > Export My Data, or email dpo@dreamscape.ai.

**What's Included**: Profile, preferences, search history, bookings, favorites, recommendations, consent records.

**Delivery**: Download link sent to your email within 24 hours (link expires in 7 days).

### 8.6 Right to Object (Article 21)

You have the right to:
- Object to processing based on legitimate interest (e.g., analytics, AI recommendations)
- Object to direct marketing at any time

**How to Exercise**:
- **Marketing**: Click "Unsubscribe" in any email, or toggle off in Settings > Notifications
- **AI Recommendations**: Toggle off in Settings > Privacy Dashboard
- **Analytics**: Manage cookie preferences in the cookie banner

**Effect**: We will stop the objected processing immediately.

### 8.7 Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7)

You have the right to:
- Withdraw consent at any time (for processing based on consent)

**How to Exercise**: Adjust toggles in Settings > Privacy Dashboard or cookie settings.

**Effect**: Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal. Some services may be limited if you withdraw essential consents.

### 8.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the right to:
- Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe we are processing your data unlawfully

**Supervisory Authority for France**:
- **CNIL** (Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertés)
- Address: 3 Place de Fontenoy, TSA 80715, 75334 Paris Cedex 07, France
- Website: https://www.cnil.fr
- Phone: +33 1 53 73 22 22

We encourage you to contact us first at dpo@dreamscape.ai so we can address your concerns.

### 8.9 Automated Decision-Making and Profiling (Article 22)

We use AI and machine learning for:
- Personalized travel recommendations
- Search result ranking
- Fraud detection

**Your Rights**:
- You can opt out of AI recommendations via Settings > Privacy Dashboard
- No solely automated decisions with legal or significant effects are made without human intervention
- You can request human review of AI-generated itineraries

**Transparency**: We provide explanations of how recommendations are generated in our Help Center.

## 9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to provide functionality, analytics, and personalization. For detailed information, see our [Cookie Policy](COOKIE_POLICY.md).

### 9.1 Types of Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies** (always active):
- `auth-storage`: JWT session management
- `XSRF-TOKEN`: Cross-site request forgery protection

**Functional Cookies** (default on, can be disabled):
- `user-preferences`: Language, currency, display settings
- `recent-searches`: Quick access to recent queries

**Analytics Cookies** (default off, requires consent):
- Page views and navigation patterns
- Search behavior and result clicks
- Feature usage and engagement metrics

**Marketing Cookies** (default off, requires consent):
- Personalized advertising
- Retargeting campaigns
- Conversion tracking

### 9.2 Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through:
- **Cookie Banner**: Appears on first visit and can be accessed anytime via footer link
- **Browser Settings**: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies
- **Privacy Dashboard**: Settings > Privacy Dashboard > Cookie Preferences

**Note**: Blocking essential cookies may prevent you from using certain features.

### 9.3 Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services:
- **Stripe**: Payment processing security
- **Mapbox**: Map rendering and location services
- **Google Analytics** (if enabled): Usage analytics

These third parties have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

## 10. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data:

### 10.1 Technical Safeguards

**Encryption**:
- **In Transit**: HTTPS with TLS 1.3 for all data transmission
- **At Rest**: AES-256 encryption for database storage
- **Passwords**: bcrypt hashing with salt (cost factor 12)

**Access Controls**:
- Role-based access control (RBAC) for internal systems
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for administrative access
- Principle of least privilege for service accounts
- JWT-based authentication with token expiration (7 days)

**Network Security**:
- Firewalls and intrusion detection systems
- API rate limiting to prevent abuse
- DDoS protection via AWS infrastructure

**Monitoring**:
- Real-time security monitoring and alerts
- Automated anomaly detection
- Comprehensive audit logging of all data access

### 10.2 Organizational Safeguards

**Staff Training**:
- Annual GDPR and security awareness training
- Background checks for employees with data access
- Confidentiality agreements

**Policies and Procedures**:
- Incident response plan with 72-hour breach notification
- Data minimization and privacy-by-design principles
- Regular security audits and penetration testing (bi-annually)

**Vendor Management**:
- Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with all processors
- Regular compliance assessments
- Contractual security requirements

### 10.3 Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach:
1. **Detection**: Immediate activation of incident response team
2. **Containment**: Affected systems isolated within hours
3. **Assessment**: Severity and impact evaluation
4. **Notification**:
   - Supervisory authority (CNIL) within 72 hours if high risk
   - Affected users without undue delay if high risk to rights and freedoms
5. **Remediation**: Root cause analysis and preventive measures

You will be notified via email with details about:
- Nature of the breach
- Likely consequences
- Measures taken to address the breach
- Recommended actions to protect yourself
